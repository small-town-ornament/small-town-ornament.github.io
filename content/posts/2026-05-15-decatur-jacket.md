I found it between two things that were trying too hard.

That is usually where the good clothes are: not displayed, not styled, not asking to be called a find. Just hanging there with enough structure to make you stop and enough wear to prove somebody had already lived a little in it.

The jacket works because it does not beg. It sharpens jeans, relaxes a dress, and makes an errand outfit look like a decision.
