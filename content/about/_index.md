Small-Town Ornament is a journal by Caroline Grace Mercer: Alabama roots, Atlanta life, movement, clothes, food, favorites, and the kind of specificity that makes a city feel lived in instead of consumed.

It is not aspirational fluff. If it was not worn, eaten, walked through, sweated in, or felt, it does not belong here.
