This is not a directory, and it is definitely not a civic service announcement.

These are the places that earned a place in my actual rotation: the bowl I would drive for, the shop where the racks still reward patience, the route that fixes my mood without pretending to fix my life. A favorite has to do more than photograph well. It has to hold up on a Tuesday, after traffic, when nobody is trying to be impressed.

I care about the practical things because practical things are part of taste: where to park, what to order, when to go, which corner to sit in, whether the room knows what it is. If a place is here, it gave me a reason to come back.
