This is the route I trust when I need my body to get a vote without turning the morning into a performance.

Piedmont Park is famous enough to be annoying, but early it still behaves. The loop from 12th Street toward the Active Oval and Lake Clara Meer gives you grass, skyline, shade, dogs with conflicting agendas, and just enough slope to make your legs report honestly. It is not a cleanse. It is not a reset. It is a way to let the day start with something other than a screen.

Go before 7:30 if you can. After that, the park becomes more available to everyone else's plans, which is its right and also my cue to leave.
