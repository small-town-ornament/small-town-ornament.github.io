The broth is the whole argument.

Not the lighting, not the tile, not whether the table photographs well. The bowl arrives and makes every overdesigned Atlanta dining room feel a little unserious. Order the rare beef, add what you need, and sit with it long enough to remember that some places do not need to perform taste because they have it.
