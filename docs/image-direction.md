# Small-Town Ornament Image Direction

All future generated image briefs for Small-Town Ornament must include:

- Shot on film, Kodak Portra 400 or similar, with visible grain.
- Natural or overcast lighting only; no studio-perfect light.
- Caroline identity anchors only for Caroline images; do not use prior generated blog art as an identity source.
- Full-frame, uncropped, vertical-safe compositions that do not cut off the top or bottom of people or meaningful objects.

For real places, use publishable real photos when rights exist. Otherwise, separate the real place reference from the Caroline identity anchor in the image brief.
